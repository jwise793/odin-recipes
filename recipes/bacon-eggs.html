Bacon & Eggs Sandwich Recipe

A bacon and egg sandwich with cheese

Ingedients

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 4 teaspoons butter, divided
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices bread

Steps

  1. Cook bacon about 7 minutes or until crispy
  2. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl
  3. Cook eggs
  4. Place bread in toaster
  5. Butter the toast
  6. Add cheese
  7. And now assemble the sandwich
